Privacy Policy

Privacy-first downloader operation.

VOIDGRAB processes URLs submitted by users to retrieve public media links. The application does not require registration, passwords, or social login credentials.

Recent downloads are stored only in the user's browser local storage and can be cleared by the user through browser controls. Server logs may contain operational metadata used for debugging, reliability, abuse prevention, and platform security.

VOIDGRAB does not sell personal information. Third-party infrastructure providers may process requests as needed to deliver the service.
